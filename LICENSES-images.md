# Image and media licensing

The Palcera Brochure template ships its demo images and brand marks inside the recipe so
the site renders completely on first install. This file records the provenance and license
of every shipped image asset, as required by the Drupal CMS marketplace rule that a
template creator **must have the legal right to distribute all shipped content**.

Every asset below is either **created for this template** (and distributed under the
package license, GPL-2.0-or-later) or a **third-party asset under a GPL-compatible
license** with attribution recorded here. None of these assets carry any third-party
rights that would restrict redistribution with the template.
